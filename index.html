HOW TO
PLAY

YOUR GOAL
Eat every glowing green square on the board.
Clear them all to breach the next sector.
You are VIRUS_X — a rogue program infecting the mainframe.
CONTROLS
WASD or ARROW KEYS to move through the maze.
SPACE fires a pulse blast — destroys any drone within range.
On mobile: swipe to move, tap to blast.
HEAT METER
The HEAT % top right shows how detected you are.
It rises when drones get close to you.
Hit 100% and you lose a life instantly.
Eating data nodes cools it back down.
DRONES
Red security drones patrol and hunt you.
Touch one = lose a life.
Blast them with SPACE — they respawn after a few seconds.
Each level adds more drones.
SUPER NODES
Yellow diamond nodes are worth 50 points each.
They also drop your heat by 8% when eaten.
Prioritise these when drones are closing in.
SCORING
Normal node = 10 pts
Super node = 50 pts
Drone blasted = 50 pts
Sector cleared = level x 200 pts
